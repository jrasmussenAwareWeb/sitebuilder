Tips & Troubleshooting

To insert the Navbar Search Box Dropdown, you cannot do so by clicking the Insert New Item icon from within the Toolbar option toolbar.


Instead, click within the Toolbar placeholder area and click the Add New button.

Restrictions & Dependencies

The Toolbar is only a placeholder for Navbar Search Box Dropdown.
