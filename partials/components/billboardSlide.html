Add Billboard Slide

To insert an billboard slide you must do so from the Billboard item —

  • Using the content tree dropdown, navigate from Billboard Slide to Billboard



  • click the Insert a new item icon

Billboard Slide Ordering


To reorder billboard slides, click the up or down arrow icons .

Billboard Slide Delete


To delete a billboard slide, click the Delete Row icon .

Billboard Slide - Add Button


To add a link button to the billboard slide, click the plus icon .

Tips & Troubleshooting

Editing Billboard Slides

If you have added more than one Billboard Slide and cannot see it in the editor, make sure you have saved.
Once saved, the editor will scroll through the slides. When the slide you wish to edit is present - click the edit icon.
Alternatively, you can reorder the slides so that the one you wish to edit is at position 1. To reorder billboard slides, click the up or down arrow icons .

Image Sizing

Size background images to a relative height and width that you wish for display prior to upload.

The above example resized appropriately from to 1410x300 results in the full background image filling the contained section.
