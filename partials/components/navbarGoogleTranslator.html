Tips & Troubleshooting

Restrictions & Dependencies

Placement

The Navbar Google Translator can only be placed within a Navbar or Navbar Jump Menu.
The Navbar has placeholders for additional items such as Navbar Logo — these can be left blank if necessary.

Column Sizing

If placing within a row, the Navbar Google Translator requires a min column sizing of 4?
