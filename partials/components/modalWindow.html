Modal Window

Allowed In

  • Accordion
  • Row
  • Row Advanced

Usage

Use a Modal Window when you want to display additional information or functionality without taking the user away from the page.
For example, a disclaimer or privacy policy link located in the footer or a navigation or other link to a register or login form.


More information at getboostrap.com
http://getbootstrap.com/javascript/#modals
