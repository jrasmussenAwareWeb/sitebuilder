Usage

Google Custom Search enables you to create a search engine for your website, your blog, or a collection of websites. You can configure your engine to search both web pages and images. You can fine-tune the ranking, add your own promotions and customize the look and feel of the search results. You can monetize the search by connecting your engine to your Google AdSense account.
https://developers.google.com/custom-search/

Tips & Troubleshooting

If the Google Search Engine ID is not entered prior to saving, the component will no longer be accessible from within the Experience Editor.
To correct this, access the component from the Content Editor, enter the ID and save.

Restrictions & Dependencies

Placement

The Google Search Results can only be placed within a Container component. Keep this in mind when planning base layouts.
